Students are responsible for ensuring AI use complies with the policy for each step.

# Step 1 – Requirements & Constraints

AI Policy:
- AI may be used for verification and clarification of requirements and constraints only.
- AI may NOT generate unverified requirements.
- Students must review and validate all AI output.
